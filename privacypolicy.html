Privacy policy


At ZIRI, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.
This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us in any other way.

We may collect the following types of personal information from you:
Name and contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
Payment and billing information, such as your credit card number and billing address.
Information about your use of our website or services, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
Other information you provide to us, such as feedback, reviews, or survey responses.

How We Use Your Information


We use your personal information for the following purposes:
To provide and improve our services, including to process payments and orders, communicate with you, and personalize your experience.
To comply with legal and regulatory requirements, such as tax and accounting obligations.
To protect our rights and property, and the safety of our users and the public.
To analyze and understand how our website and services are used, and to develop and improve our products and marketing strategies.

Information Sharing and Disclosure


We may share your personal information with the following third parties:
Service providers and vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processors and shipping companies.
Legal and regulatory authorities, as required by law or in response to a court order or subpoena.
Other third parties with your consent or at your direction.
We may also disclose your information in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business assets.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies


We use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of our website and services. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other usage data. We use this information to personalize your experience and to analyze and improve our website and services.

Your Choices and Rights


You have the following choices and rights regarding your personal information:
You may opt-out of receiving marketing emails from us by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email or by contacting us at our email.
You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us at our email.
You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal and regulatory obligations, by contacting us at our email.

Security


We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy


We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and providing notice via email or other means.
